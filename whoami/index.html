Who Am I?

01 / Movement

Dance

In the dance of life I confidently create and decisively lead Feeling the music And breathing the beat

02 / Voice

Speak to the room

My voice a dynamic melody Whispers in every ear Weaving arcs for my characters Transforming all that hear

03 / Beliefs

Stand rooted

I stand for my beliefs Rooted like a tree I draw my standards in the crease I do not people please

04 / Joy

Beams in the joy of now

I am he that beams In the joy of now No reason needed Just the joy of now

05 / Ideas

Turn thought into wealth

I spin ideas and My ideas money invite In the game of riches I shine so bright
